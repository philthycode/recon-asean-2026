About the Conference

Building an Inclusive Future Through Solidarity

As the Philippines assumes the ASEAN Chairmanship in 2026, the National Anti-Poverty Commission (NAPC) convenes this landmark regional forum to advance the ASEAN Social and Solidarity Economy (SSE) SDG Roadmap 2026–2030.

Bringing together government leaders, international development practitioners, UN agencies, and social enterprise champions from across ASEAN and beyond, this conference marks the Philippines' official launchpad for transitioning poverty alleviation from social welfare to inclusive economic integration.
